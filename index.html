About

I’m a 3D Artist with over three years of experience producing high-end visualizations for both interior and exterior architecture using 3ds Max, Corona Renderer, and Photoshop. I also integrate AI tools like ComfyUI to enhance both creativity and production efficiency.

What drives my work is visual storytelling that combines technical precision with emotional depth. I aim to create imagery that not only meets architectural standards but also evokes mood and narrative.

I bring hands-on experience working onsite and remotely across large-scale projects, sharpening adaptability, collaboration, and problem-solving. My strengths lie in lighting, materials, and cinematic composition—skills I use to produce visuals that resonate artistically and professionally.
