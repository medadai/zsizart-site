About

Mahmoud Refaey — 3D Artist & ArchViz

I’m a 3D Artist and Architectural Visualizer specializing in photorealistic interior and exterior imagery. I work primarily with 3ds Max and Corona Renderer, and I integrate AI tools such as ComfyUI to accelerate look-dev and expand creative exploration. My process blends cinematic composition, precise lighting, and material fidelity to deliver visuals that are both technically accurate and emotionally engaging.

I’ve delivered work for residential, hospitality, and mixed-use developments—onsite and remotely—collaborating smoothly with architects, designers, and developers. Core strengths include lighting direction, material realism, and color grading, taking projects from early concept to polished marketing imagery with consistent quality and reliability.
