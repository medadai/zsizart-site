About

ZSIZART is an independent visualization studio led by Mahmoud Refaey. We specialize in crafting cinematic architectural imagery and animation — blending composition, light, and material realism to create work that feels immersive and emotionally engaging.

We partner with architects, developers, and design brands worldwide, supporting them from concept through to polished marketing assets. Our focus: clarity, reliability, and visuals that inspire confidence in every project.
